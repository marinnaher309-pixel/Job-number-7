About Cumilla Polytechnic institute

Comilla Polytechnic Institute is a government technical and vocational polytechnic institute in Comilla, Bangladesh, established in 1962. It is one of the oldest and largest polytechnic institutes in the country, located in a hilly area near Shalban Vihara in Kotbari. The institute is managed by the Bangladesh Technical Education Board and offers a variety of courses in fields like Architecture, Electrical, and Civil Engineering, as well as other fields like Accountancy and Mass Communication. Key details Name: Comilla Polytechnic Institute Location: Kotbari, Comilla, Chittagong Division, Bangladesh Established: 1962 Managed by: Bangladesh Technical Education Board EIIN Number: 133160 Programs and courses Disciplines: Offers a variety of courses in technical and vocational fields.
